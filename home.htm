A game inspired by the study of psychology and the nuances of the mind, Fever Dreams is a dynamic world full of mystery. Try to keep your sanity rating above zero as you adventure along in the xxxx custom-built rooms available, all of which will change based on your character's current mental health. Stumble around in a reality of your choosing by managing your sanity levels, discovering things that only those too far gone from the  norm can see, hear, taste or feel. Keeping sane is not your only challenge; you will find as you sleep you will be pulled into the dream world at random, facing bizarre scenarios such as fighting your third grade teacher using checkers, intimate moments with fantasies, or unspeakable nightmares that will haunt your waking hours. Will you be able to tell what is real and what isn't?
